This app is for keeping track of one's own individual habits (health or work-related, anything that person does everyday, or strives to do everyday). The main thing is the so called system has to be very important to the person.

My systems include:

  1. Sleep (10-12 pm to 6-8 am)
  2. Food (Natural whole foods 80% of the time, 3-4 meals a day)
  3. Active (Legs, Rest, Upper, Run, Sprint, Upper, Jog)
  4. Sunlight (Morning sunlight)
  5. Hygiene (skin and oral care, shower after training, home cleaning, laundry)
  6. Work (at least 4 hours of focused work, time block)
  7. Read (Read at least for a hour a day)
  8. Write (Plan for tomorrow, anything)
